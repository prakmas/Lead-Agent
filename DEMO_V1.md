# DEMO — Version 1

This is **Version 1** of our app — the first working demo. Below is a short, simple
script to present it confidently.

---

## What this app is (say this first)
> "This is a smart WhatsApp assistant for a business. When a customer messages on
> WhatsApp, it understands what they want, replies instantly like a real agent,
> finds matching options from our list, and keeps following up automatically — all
> managed from one admin dashboard."

---

## What to show (live, step by step)

**1. Send a WhatsApp message**
From a phone, message the business number:
> `need a 2bhk in koramangala under 30000`

**2. Show the instant reply**
The bot replies in seconds with matching options. Say:
> "Notice — it replied on its own, with real options from our database."

**3. Open the admin dashboard**
Go to the dashboard → **Inbox**. Show:
- The customer's chat appears here live
- Their details (name, number) are saved automatically
- You can **reply by hand** too, or **turn the bot off** and chat yourself

**4. Show the other pages (quick)**
- **Leads** — every customer request is captured
- **Listings** — the options the bot matches against
- **Matches** — what the bot recommended and why

---

## Key points to mention (say these)
- ✅ **Real-time** — replies in seconds, automatically.
- ✅ **Real data** — the options shown are from our database, not made up by AI.
- ✅ **Never miss a lead** — every message is saved as a lead with full history.
- ✅ **Human + bot** — staff can jump in and reply manually anytime.
- ✅ **Always on** — it runs in the cloud 24/7, no laptop needed.
- ✅ **Auto follow-up** — it keeps sending new matches until the customer says done.

---

## If someone asks…
- **"Does it work for any business?"** → "Yes — real estate, rentals, services.
  We just change the listings."
- **"Is the AI making up answers?"** → "No. AI only understands the message; the
  options come from our real database."
- **"Can anyone message it?"** → "In this demo it's a test number for a few
  numbers. With a verified business number, the whole public can chat with it."

---

## One honest line about Version 1
> "This is our first version — fully working end to end. Next we'll move to a real
> business number so any customer can use it, and add more dashboard features."
